All About Me

My name is Saba Hassani, and I am a passionate photographer with an obsession for preserving life’s fleeting moments. While I specialize in fashion and portrait photography, I also find immense joy in capturing the beauty of landscapes and societies, revealing the extraordinary in the everyday.

"Turning ordinary moments into extraordinary memories." This belief shapes my professional work ethic. I find it deeply significant to capture seemingly common moments with a fresh, artistic perspective that transforms them into something truly captivating.

I hold a degree in Psychology, a background that has profoundly enriched my photography. Over time, I have honed my skills to become a professional photographer, and I’ve witnessed the unique impact my psychological insights have on my clients and models. Understanding human emotions and behavior helps me create a comfortable environment and bring out authentic expressions in every shot.

I have earned several certifications from the Tehran Institute of Technology, specializing in general photography and photo retouching. Additionally, I have acquired certifications from the Ministry of Culture and Islamic Guidance in portrait and commercial photography. My expertise also extends to videography, for which I completed a course at the same institute. I had the privilege of gaining hands-on experience during a year-long membership with the Young Photography Club (YPHC).

My photographic style is minimalistic, focusing on simplicity and emotional depth. I draw inspiration from my surroundings, often experimenting with colors to create extraordinary visuals. I believe in making every photoshoot an enjoyable and memorable experience for my clients and models.

As a photographer, I strive to achieve ambitious goals, including collaborating with renowned companies and magazines. Ultimately, I aspire to establish my own photography studio—a space where creativity knows no bounds.

''Feel free to reach out for collaborations. Together, we can create professional yet fun memories that last a lifetime.''
